Global Analysis of Inflation, Unemployment, and CO2 Emissions: Understanding Interdependencies (2014-2024)

High inflation can erode the purchasing power of consumers, potentially leading to reduced demand for goods and services, which in turn affects job markets. By studying this relationship, policymakers can better understand how to manage inflation without creating spikes in unemployment, thus ensuring social stability.
